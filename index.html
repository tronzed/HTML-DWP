About Us

I am a Surrey and Berkshire wedding photographer, established in 2005 and proud to have been entrusted to document the wedding day of over 500 happy couples, that’s a lot of weddings.

Wedding photography is exciting! Meeting new couples, each with their own story to tell and then being given the opportunity to record the raw emotion and excitement of their wedding day. The funny moments, the tears and everything in between, there really is no other occasion when these heightened emotions are felt in such abundance.
